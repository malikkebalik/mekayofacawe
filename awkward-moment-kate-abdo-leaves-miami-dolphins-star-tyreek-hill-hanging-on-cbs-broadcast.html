Awkward moment Kate Abdo leaves Miami Dolphins star Tyreek Hill hanging on CBS broadcast

 Posted on July 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  263 words  |  Aldo Pusey

We've all been there - but for Miami Dolphins star Tyreek Hill, it happened live on TV.

The wide receiver joined CBS' special Champions League broadcast in Miami alongside ex-Premier League stars Thierry Henry, Clint Dempsey and Micah Richards on Tuesday night.

But it was host Kate Abdo who unintentionally provided the most memorable moment of Hill's appearance.

Right at the end, Hill shook Henry's hand and turned to Abdo, holding his hand outstretched for seconds.

It became painfully clear that Abdo's attention was elsewhere and the NFL superstar was awkwardly forced to shake his own hand.

Abdo tried to salvage the situation, but it was too late and the damage was already done. We all saw.

"I played soccer all the way up until middle school, but my mum was like, 'Hey you're so good at American football, that I want you to only stick to American football.' I was good at soccer." Hill said.

"I was supposed to be playing soccer right now but my mum wanted me to play football, because football is a part of our household and everyone in our family played football.

"I was the different one and I wanted to play soccer.
