Baywatchs Erika Eleniak Shows Off Tattoos On Rare Public Outing Hollywood Life

 Posted on August 9, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  191 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Erika Eleniak stepped out for a rare public appearance in California on Sunday. The former Baywatch star, 53, who played Shauni McClain on the hit show, looked incredible as she showed off her gorgeous body art, including two full sleeves of tattoos, as seen in photos here, via Page Six. The stunning blonde rocked a maroon tee and cute patchwork pants during her hike with her adorable puppy.

Adding a plethora of necklaces, Erica kept a low-key style as she enjoyed the sunny outing in a pair of flip-flops. With her trademark locks parted in the middle and left long and loose, the Glendale native was instantly recognizable, and looked happy and healthy while at it.

It was quite a treat for fans, as Erika has been keeping out of the spotlight a bit recently. Although she did take to her Instagram in May to share a stunning photo of herself and to ask her fans to try a bit of self-care. “Wishing everyone a very happy ‘Self-Care Sunday,'” she wrote. “What are you doing for YOU? I will be spending time with family- So good for the soul. #selfcaresunday.”
