Brief About TwoSet  Violin

TwoSet Violin is a comedy pair comprising Australian violinists,  Brett Yang and Eddy Chen. The duo is nicely-known for their musical comedy on their Youtube channel.

As of 202, their channel has reached more than 3.5 million subscribers and around 1.07 billion views as of February 2022. Yang and Chen met in mathematics tuition class. They trained as the youngest members of a youth orchestra.

Later they began to post their cover video of pop music played on the violin on their YouTube channel in 2013. Later, they left the Queensland Symphony Orchestra and Sydney Symphony Orchestra so that they could start to perform their own live concerts.

On 8 September 2016, they revealed their live performance in Brisbane, Australia through Youtube. The show was mostly violin-based with a comedy format and less in a traditional concert.

Initially, they did a street performance in Sydney and collected funds to go on a world tour in 2017 to 11 cities in 10 countries, in Asia and Europe comprising Frankfurt, Taipei, and Helsinki.

They also conducted programs in some places of the United States like New York City, Los Angeles, and San Francisco.

Though they declared their next worldwide tour in October 2019, it was postponed due to the COVID-19 pandemic and the new date is not revealed yet. But, the virtual world tour event was performed on 28 December 2021.

It also has a clothing line named TwoSet Apparel.
