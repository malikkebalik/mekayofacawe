Has Paige Spiranac changed her sport? Golf influencer sends fans into frenzy: 'We ALL won'

 Posted on July 28, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  409 words  |  Aldo Pusey

MONUMENT, COLORADO: Paige Spiranac, a golf influencer appears to have changed sports recently, which is driving fans insane. Her recent captivating photograph sparked a fan frenzy over her attire. After swapping the golf course for a basketball court, the golfer-turned-influencer managed to impress her 3.7 million fans.

Spiranac is known for her flaunting her figure and being unapologetic about her sex appeal. This has resulted in her receiving criticism for her clothes, particularly on the greens when she was a pro golfer. The remarks continued even after she gave up being a professional golfer to pursue a career in social media as a golf sport influencer instead. She stated that one of the reasons she stopped playing pro golf was the harsh judgements by other players about her fashion choices. She even posted a spoof video of her trying out the career of a stripper because she keeps getting asked, "Are you a stripper or a golfer?"

'Has Paige Spiranac changed her sport?'

In her most recent post, Spiranac was seen on the basketball court, taking photos with an NBA-branded Spalding ball. Posting the photos on Instagram, she captioned them, "Gotta love NBA finals Nuggets take game 1! Who do you think will end up winning?"

Spiranac stood for the camera while holding a basket ball under her arm. She was dressed in a grey sports bra and pink hipsters in the photo. Her hair was flawlessly done in a high ponytail, and she donned white sports shoes to complete the sporty outfit.

'We ALL won with this post'

Many fans responded in kind. A fan wrote, "We ALL won with this post." Another added, "One clear winner is you Paige !!" Some of the golf  fans wrote wrote, "Sorry, not an NBA fan!" Another one added. "Couldn’t care less. Basketball is a horrendously boring game. I’d rather watch grass grow."

While other was eager to point out Paige Spiranac's unmarried status despite her stunning appearance. He wrote, "How is there not a ring on that finger!??" Spiranac, continues to express herself on all social media channels. She discusses current golf events and has amassed a significant fan base over the years. So the basketball post seems like a one-off for now and there is no official announcement of her changing her sport. She will continue to influence golfers though social media but might dip into other popular sports like basketball once in a while as her recent post indicates.
