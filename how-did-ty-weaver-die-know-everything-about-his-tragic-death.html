Ty Weaver, a 50-year-old staff member at St. Petersburg’s 3 Daughters Brewing, passed away tragically after being involved in a fatal car accident on early Thursday morning, March 2, 2023. His unfortunate passing has come as a complete shock to everyone, and the entire community is mourning the death of Ty Weaver. Keep reading this article to learn more about the late Ty Weaver, what happened to him, how he died, and much more!

 
Who was Ty Weaver? What happened to him?

Ty Weaver was a 50-year-old staff member at St. Petersburg’s popular brewery, “3 Daughters Brewing,” in the United States. He had been a part of the brewery since its founding and has served as the head brewer. His work also included working at the bar. His co-workers described him as a very private person.

The 50-year-old brewer, Ty Weaver, was involved in a tragic accident on the Sunshine Skyway Bridge on March 2, 2023. According to reports, he was riding a motorcycle when he unfortunately collided with an SUV in the northbound lanes at about 9:15 a.m. on Thursday, March 2.

How did Ty Weaver die? Who announced his death?

St. Petersburg’s 3 Daughters Brewing’s head brewer, Ty Weaver, passed away unexpectedly after being involved in a tragic accident on Thursday, March 2, 2023. He suffered several injuries during the accident and was transported to a nearby hospital, where he later died. The news of St. Petersburg’s 3 Daughters Brewing’s Ty Weaver was confirmed by the owner of the company, Mike Harting.

While announcing the news of Ty Weaver’s tragic death, no information about his funeral ceremony was announced by any of his family members or friends. Further details about his funeral are expected to be released very soon.

Tributes pour in over Ty Weaver’s death 

Following the news of Ty Weaver’s unfortunate death, netizens started paying their tributes and condolences to the late brewer across different social media platforms. St. Petersburg’s 3 Daughters Brewing also shared a tribute statement for the late Ty Weaver. It reads, “The 3 Daughters Brewing Family is really overwhelmed by the tremendous amount of love and support from the community for the unfortunate passing of our head brewer, Ty Weaver.”

“Ty was an exceptional person whose talent, dedication, and love for the art of brewing were absolutely unmatched. He will be dearly missed by his friends and the community surrounding 3 Daughters. We respectfully ask for privacy for his family during this time of grief,” the brewery said.

We share our deepest condolences with the family members, friends, and all loved ones of the late Ty Weaver. We pray to the almighty to give everyone the strength to pass through this difficult time period. His passing is a huge loss for the entire 3 Daughters community, but he will be remembered for his great work.
