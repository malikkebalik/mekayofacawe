Born on June 20, 2001, Goncalo Matias Ramos is a Portuguese professional soccer player who plays as a forward for Benfica and represents the Portugal national team.

The Portuguese player emerged into the limelight during the 2022 World Cup when he replaced Cristiano Ronaldo in the game against Switzerland in the round of 16, scoring a hat-trick.
