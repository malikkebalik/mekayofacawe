Jerome Tang Career

Tang was the head coach at Heritage Christian School in Cleveland, Texas, from 1993 to 2003, and he helped the club be a strong competitor. He mentored future NBA star Von Wafer at Heritage Christian. Tang joined Baylor as an assistant coach in 2003, under first-year coach Scott Drew. Tang was promoted to assistant head coach in 2017, and he guided Baylor to winning the national title in 2021.
