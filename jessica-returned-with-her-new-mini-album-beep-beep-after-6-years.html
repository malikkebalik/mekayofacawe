Jessica Returned With Her New Mini-Album &quot;BEEP BEEP&quot; After 6 Years

 Posted on August 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  449 words  |  Larita Shotwell

With the release of her fourth mini-album, “BEEP BEEP,” K-pop icon Jessica will end the six years since the release of “My Decade” in 2018 and make a sensational return. The star has now dropped the bombshell, confirming that the album would be released on the 22nd as was widely speculated.

Jessica Release Mini-Album “BEEP BEEP”

The album “BEEP BEEP” is sure to be a musical delight for its listeners, since it contains not one but six tracks, one of which is the album’s namesake. Jessica’s new mini album BEEP BEEP will be released on Nov 22, 2023.

Anticipation will be built with the gradual unveiling of concept photographs, music video teasers, and album samplers in the weeks leading up to the big comeback. Jessica’s devoted fanbase will likely view the announcement of a new mini-album as nothing short of a Christmas miracle.

Her 2016 debut album “With Love, J” marked the beginning of Jessica’s solo career, and since then she has won fans over with songs like “Fly,” “WONDERLAND,” and “Summer Storm.” Her digital singles have piqued fans’ interest, but a full six-song mini-album is what has really had them excited.

Jessica Announces ‘BEEP BEEP’ Release and Concerts in Singapore and Macau

Jessica’s previous albums have always served as canvases for her varied musical tastes, and “BEEP BEEP” looks to be no exception. Everyone is wondering what hue Jessica will choose to create her next musical masterpiece. The album’s release is a precursor to actual performances. Jessica is preparing to perform in Singapore and Macau, giving her dedicated fan base a rare opportunity to meet her in person.
