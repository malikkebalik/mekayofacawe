Early Life

Joshua Patrick Allen was born on May 21, 1996, in Firebaugh, California, where he spent his formative years on a 3,000-acre cotton farm. Despite his undeniable talent as a high school football standout, Allen initially struggled to attract attention from prominent college football programs. However, his perseverance led him to enroll at Reedley College, eventually earning a transfer to the University of Wyoming.

Josh Allen Contract

In 2018, Josh Allen’s dreams became reality when he was selected as the seventh overall pick by the Buffalo Bills in the NFL draft. His journey with the Bills began with a fully guaranteed four-year, $21 million contract, setting the stage for his professional career.
