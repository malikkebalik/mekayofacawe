Jurassic World 3: Dominion Release Date

In the United States and the United Kingdom, Jurassic World Dominion will be released on June 10, 2022.

Jurassic World 3: Dominion Cast & Crew

Jurassic World 3 brings together the casts of the World and Jurassic Park films, serving as a sort of conclusion to the Jurassic World trilogy thus far.

As a result, Chris Pratt, Bryce Dallas Howard, Justice Smith, Daniela Pineda, and Omar Sy reprise their iconic roles from the original Jurassic films, while Laura Dern, Sam Neil, and Jeff Goldblum repeat their major roles from the World films.

BD Wong, DeWanda Wise, Mamoudou Athie, Scott Haze, and Campbell Scott round out the cast.

Jurassic World 3: Dominion Plot

As Dominion succeeds the events of Shattered Kingdom, dinosaurs have now escaped across the continent. Trevorrow has stated that Dern, Neil, and Goldblum are as essential to the film as Pratt and Howard, and disclosed in a conversation with Collider that the plot follows two parallel paths that are predestined to intersect:

“You are presenting two parallel storylines that are driving more and closer together, and you recognize it, and you begin to comprehend how they will meet, and then they do. However, this is not the conventional approach to building a film.

The way we’ve been constructing it, moving things about, and trying to figure it out has made it a lot less of a given than if we had followed the typical screenplay format, where everyone knows what will happen on page twelve.”

But Trevorrow also hinted at a global adventure, adding that the picture is a “spy drama adventure” with “a touch of Bourne and Bond” put in. While he has not yet disclosed all of the film’s locales, he has announced that the United Kingdom, British Columbia, and Malta would serve as the film’s key settings.
