Kagiso Rabada Wikipedia

Kagiso Rabada Wikipedia:- Kagiso Rabada was born on 25 May 1995 in Johannesburg, Gauteng, South Africa. He is a South African international cricketer who plays all formats of the game. He is a right-arm fast bowler. He made his international debut in limited-overs cricket in November 2014, followed by his Test debut in November 2015. By January 2018, he had topped both the ICC ODI and Test bowling rankings at the age of 22. In July 2018, he became the youngest bowler in Test history to take 150 wickets.
