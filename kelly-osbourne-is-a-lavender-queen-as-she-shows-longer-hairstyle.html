Kelly Osbourne Is a 'Lavender Queen' as She Shows Longer Hairstyle

 Posted on September 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  341 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Kelly Osbourne debuts a stunning longer hairstyle with beautiful lavender locks and receives plenty of compliments from her fans. Yet, the media personality reveals she wasn't always praised for her hair color.

Kelly Osbourne recently took to social media to flaunt her new hairstyle and makeup, a creation of Kip Zachary and Laura Rugetti. In her Instagram post, Kelly is seen in a black top with her lavender-purple hair tied half up in a voluminous black bun.

Kelly's locks gently curl down her shoulders, appearing longer and thicker, courtesy of her hairstylist's use of hair extensions. She captioned her photo, "I feel the lavender haze creepin' up on me."

Ozzy Osbourne's daughter received a shower of compliments from her fans on the photo of her new look. One fan proclaimed that she is undoubtedly the "Lavender Queen" with this color. Another agreed that she looks pretty in purple.

One of the commenters declares that Kelly is the most beautiful. "You invented the color purple, my favorite girl," they added.

Kelly Osbourne with father Ozzy Osbourne, 2014 | Source: Getty Images

However, Kelly Osbourne's journey with purple hair hasn't always been smooth. She recalls dying her hair this color long before it became a trend, and not everyone appreciated the bold shade. Critics claimed it made Kelly appear older or even likened her to an outcast.

Nevertheless, Osbourne confesses she always loved it. When she first dyed her hair, her mother, Sharon Osbourne, wanted her to revert to her natural golden color. Yet, Sharon later conceded that her daughter's happiness was paramount. Since then, she has refrained from commenting on Kelly's hair color.

Now, Kelly confidently showcases every change in her hairstyle, such as in 2021, when she showed her hair dyed in a gradient of purple shades, from dark to light.

Kelly's social media updates are not limited to her style transformations. She recently celebrated her first Mother's Day in the role of a mother, having welcomed her first child at the end of 2022 with her boyfriend, Sid Wilson.
