Kurupt is an American rapper and actor. He was born and raised in Philidelphia and relocated to California, where he met Snoop Dogg and Daz Dillinger. He started his rap career in California with the duo Tha Dogg Pound in the 1990s. Kurupt has appeared on west-coast legendary works such as All Eyes on Me, Doggystyle, and The Chronic, all released by Deathrow Records. Then he moved to Anta Records, based out of his hometown, Philidelphia. He released 3 albums with that record, which outsold each other as released. He went back to Deathrow Records in 2006, as the Executive Vice President of the company (now Tha Records). Kurupt is active on Instagram with more than 350k followers.

Girlfriend / Spouse

Kurupt has dated –

  • Foxy Brown (1996-2000) – Inga Fung Marchand, better known Foxy Brown is an American rapper, model and actress who started dating Foxy in 1996. They got engaged in 1999. But later that year, their relationship started turning sour as Foxy allegedly had an affair with the rapper DMX. This occurred during the pair’s recording of Dog & a Fox in the album Chyna Doll (1999). Although both DMX and Foxy denied the allegations, Kurupt released a diss track titled, Callin’ Out Names targetting the couple. Eventually, after the murder of Dwayne “Draws” Dupree, Foxy and Kurupt’s relationship went down the drain after a shooting outside Kurupt’s studio at Los Angeles. DMX was a suspect in this. Foxy and Kurupt then officially announced their break up in 2000.
  • Natina Reed (2001-2003) – Natina was an American singer-songwriter, rapper, and actress who in the early 2000s got engaged with Kurupt but never got married. They planned to get married in 2001 spring, but due to their work schedule conflicts, it was pushed to 2002 or 2003. Foxy, Kurupt’s ex dissed Natina on the track 730 from her 2001 album Broken Silence, to which Reed said that she had no interest in having feuds with people. Natina and Kurupt had a child named Tren Brown (b. 2002) during the recording of Blaque Out. Shortly after that, Kurupt and Natina split in 2003. On October 26, 2012, Natina Reed died in a traffic collision.
  • Gail Gotti (2007-2017) – Gail is an American rapper whose relationship with Kurupt was rather private when they got married in 2007. They got divorced in 2017. Gail alleged that Kurupt left her because she had developed cancer. Kurupt denied these accusations and claimed that it just wasn’t working out.

    • Note – Kurupt has 2 daughters named Mary Brown and Morgan Brown. The name of their mother has been kept private.
