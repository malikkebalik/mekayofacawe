Leonardo Baldwin is an American celebrity kid who has grabbed paparazzi since his birthdate. Well, he is famous for being the third child of Oscar winner actor Alec Baldwin and yoga instructor Hilaria Baldwin. His dad, Alec first received popularity after starring on  CBS television drama Knots Landing in season 6 and seven.

He was born on September 12, 2016, in New York City as Leonardo Angel Charles Baldwin. During his birth time, his mother posted the photo of her with the newborn child on Instagram saying: "We are so pleased to introduce you to our new baby". His uncles are Stephen Baldwin, Daniel Baldwin, William Baldwin and grandparents are Carol M. Baldwin(died on 15 April 1983), Alexander Baldwin, 90.

Leonardo's Four Biological Siblings & A Half-Sister

Three years old Leonardo Angel is now growing under his parents' shadow. So he is often captured enjoying his parents and siblings. 

Altogether, he has four siblings, one sister and three brothers. All of them are under the age of eight. They are Carmen Gabriela Baldwin, 7, Rafael Thomas Baldwin, 5, Romeo Alejandro David Baldwin, 2.  His youngest brother Eduardo Pau Lucas Baldwin was welcomed on September 8, 2020. 

But his mother, Hilaria was suffered from a miscarriage during the fourth month of pregnancy in November 2019.

The five children of Baldwin now lives under the same roof along with their parents by sharing happiness and tears together. Not only this, but he has also an older half-sister Ireland Baldwin, from her dad's previous marriage. 

Alec & Hilaria's 8 Years of Nuptials

His mom Hilaria, 36, and dad, Alec, 62 had their first interaction in February 2011 at New York's restaurant Pure Food and Wine. Then they continue chatting through phones and finally had their first date at a theatre district restaurant. 

Despite their huge age difference, they romantically started dating in Augst 2011. The two moved to Greenwich Village from Upper West Side. After a year in April 2012, the duo became engaged.

On Saturday, June 30, 2012, the yoga teacher and Knots Landing's star exchanged the wedding vows at the Basilica of St. Patrick’s Old Cathedral in New York. The ceremony was officiated by Msgr. George T. Deas. In 2020, they have celebrated the 8th marriage anniversary.

Dad, Baldwin's First Marriage With Kim Basinger

Yes before marrying Hilaria, the son of Carol Martineau Baldwin and the late Alexander R. Baldwin Jr. was married to actress, Kim Basinger, 66. They first met on the set of the film The Marrying Man in 1991.

The former spouses got hitched on 19 August 1993 in East Hampton. But after nine years later, they ended their union on 3 February 2002. From their togetherness, they had a daughter Ireland, born on born on October 23, 1995.

 As of now, Ireland is known as a fashion model and actress.
