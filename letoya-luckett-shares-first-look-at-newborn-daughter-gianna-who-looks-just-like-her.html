LeToya Luckett Shares First Look At Newborn Daughter Gianna Who Looks Just Like Her

 Posted on July 20, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  459 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Former Destiny’s Child member LeToya Luckett has finally given fans their first full look at her infant daughter, Gianna and it’s genuinely too cute for words.

Before now, LeToya, 37, has been sharing glimpses of Gianna, including her hands and a full head of hair.

In a photo shared to Instagram on Monday, the singer/actress finally unveiled her precious child’s face.

The image, taken from a photoshoot, shows little Gianna catching up on her beauty sleep while propped up in a hot air balloon decorated with pink, white, and green flowers.

“Gianna,” LeToya simply captioned the picture, adding a flower emoji.

And it appears Miss Gianna does not joke with snooze time, because in several other photos taken from the shoot and shared to the official Instagram page of LeToya and her husband, Tommicus "Tommi" Walker, the little mama assumed various poses, all while asleep.

"This is a straight up blessing, just a blessing. So keep us in your prayers."

The caption on one post read: “Babies are blessings from God.”

Fans seemed to think so, too, as they trooped to LeToya’s comment section to gush over her mini-me and point out the striking resemblance.

LeToya and Tommi, 38, welcomed their bundle of joy on January 4. The singer shared their good news on Instagram one day later with a black-and-white image of the newborn’s hand intertwined with her parents.

“Gianna Iman walker,” LeToya wrote, announcing their daughter’s full name while also adding that she weighed 7lbs 15oz at birth.

Gianna is LeToya’s first child, but Tommi has a six-year-old daughter, Madison, from a previous relationship.

It was Madison who first announced last June that her father and stepmom were expecting a baby.

“Daddy and Miss Toya sitting in a tree. K-I-S-S-I-N-G. First comes love, then comes marriage, then comes the baby,” she sang in a cute Instagram video that ended with her holding up several ultrasound pictures.

At the time, the “Back 2 Life” singer later told ESSENCE in an interview:

“We are over the moon and overjoyed by this news. We feel so blessed that God has gifted us with the gift of life. …This is a straight up blessing, just a blessing. So keep us in your prayers. It doesn’t matter if it’s a girl or boy. We just wanted to have a healthy baby.”

LeToya revealed last October that they were expecting a girl.

The “Greenleaf” actress and Tommi, a Dallas-based businessman, tied the knot in December 2017 after dating for four months.
