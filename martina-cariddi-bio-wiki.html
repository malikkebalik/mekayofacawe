Who is Martina Cariddi?

Martina Cariddi is a Spanish actress who is known for her in movies like ‘The Invisible Guardian’, ‘While at War’, and ‘Elite’.

Furthermore, she has appeared on several other television series. Currently, she is busy with her important role in the Tv Series, Elite.

What are the birth facts of Martina Cariddi?

Martina Cariddi was born to her parents on May 30, 2001, in Chamartín, Madrid, Spain. Her full name is Martina Basanta Cariddi. As of now, she is 20 years old.

Likewise, her father is Eduardo Basanta Cariddi and her mother is Angela Cariddi, a journalist. She grew up in her hometown with her brother, Walter Basanta Cariddi. Moreover, she can fluently speak Spanish and Italian language.

She has a mixed ethnicity of Spanish and Italian and holds Spanish citizenship. Further, her birth sign is Gemini.

Education, University

Martina was enrolled at a local private school and later she joined the Municipal School of Dramatic Art in Madrid.

After that, she trained as an actor from Cristina Rota Center and Estudio Juan Codina.

Martina Cariddi: Career, Professional Life

Having started her career with minor roles in different shows and small programs later she debuted from the film ‘The Invisible Garden’ in 2017 followed by tv series ‘Cuéntame’ in 2018.

After that, there is no looking backward and has appeared in more than 5 films to date.

Some of the most notable films she has played include While at War, La Tarotista, Muere Padre Muere, and this was followed by tv series, Elite Short Stories: Patrick

Currently, she is appearing with her role of Mencía Blanco in the Tv Series, Elite. She has gained huge popularity from her performance in the series.
