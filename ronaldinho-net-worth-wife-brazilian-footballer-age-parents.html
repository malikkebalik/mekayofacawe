Ronaldinho Net Worth- Early Life/Career

Early Life & Career: - Ronaldinho began his career with the Grêmio youth team. During the 1998 Copa Libertadores, he made his senior team debut. Ronaldinho signed a five-year contract with French club Paris Saint-Germain in 2001 for €5 million. On 4 August 2001, Ronaldinho made his league debut for the club, coming on as a substitute in a 1-1 tie with Auxerre. Despite frequent squabbles with Fernández, Ronaldinho returned to the squad for the 2002-03 season, wearing the number 10 jersey. On September 28, Ronaldinho scored his first goal for Milan in a 1-0 derby triumph against Inter Milan. On October 19, he scored his first brace in a 3-0 win over Sampdoria.

He won his first cap for Brazil on June 26, three days before the commencement of the 1999 Copa América, in a 3-0 win against Latvia, and he scored one goal during Brazil's winning Copa América campaign. In March 2020, he was questioned by authorities in Paraguay after allegedly using a fraudulent passport to enter the nation for a charity event and book promotion, with Ronaldinho and his brother both being detained in the country.
