Sianney Garcia is an American singer, rapper, TV personality, songwriter, and social media star best known as one half of the pop, rap/hip-hop duo, SiAngie Twins with her twin sister Angelise Garcia. At the encouragement of their older brother, Danny Garcia, a well-known boxer and the positive response of their 1st music performance at one of their brother’s engagement party, the two created a YouTube channel on December 5, 2011. While they did cover a number of well-known songs by Drake and Emeli Sande, Sianney and Angelise came to prominence with their debut single, After School, released in July 2013, which was re-released in 2014. Due to a high interest in what they could do, in 2014, she and her sister also went on to release a track named Butterflies and Can’t Wait for Christmas as well as their debut EP, My All in 2014. The album consisted of tracks Girlfriend, Kids In The Basement, Long Way Home, Don’t Understand, One Kiss, To the Top, and Never Break My Heart.

After tracks named Cali Swag and Tonight in collaboration with Jalen Hoang, released in 2015, SiAngie Twins also began branching out through new content for their YouTube channel. Those included challenges such as egg roulette, smoothie, twin telepathy, and connecting with their fanbase through storytime and Q&A videos. While they only released 1 single in 2016 titled Ashame ft. Malu Trevejo, the year 2017 was more prolific and saw the release of singles Want Me Back, Cardi, and Savage. SiAngie Twins’ busiest year came in 2018 and saw the release of their 2nd EP, Innocent Savages as well as a track such as Splash ft. DreamDoll. Sianney and her sister also released songs such as Crazy Love, Forever, and Watch Me Go featuring Miky Woodz in 2019.

    As a Singer

    Sianney Garcia, as part of the duo SiAngie Twins with her twin sister Angelise Garcia, released her debut single titled After School 1st in July 2013, which was then re-released on all major platforms in 2014. It was followed closely by their 2nd single titled Butterflies as well as their original Christmas anthem titled Can’t Wait for Christmas. In 2014, Sianney and her sister also released their debut EP, My All, which consisted of tracks titled Long Way Home, Don’t Understand, One Kiss, To the Top, Girlfriend, Kids In The Basement, and Never Break My Heart.

    The year of 2015 saw the release of their track named Cali Swag as well as being featured artists on a track named Tonight by Jalen Hoang, while in 2016 they only released a track named Ashame, also featuring Malu Trevejo. In 2017, she and her sister Angelise released 3 new tracks, Savage, Cardi, and Want Me Back, in preparation for the upcoming EP. SiAngie Twins released their 2nd EP, Innocent Savages on June 8, 2018, which consisted of 6 tracks – Sazón, Want Me Back (Remix), Show Me, Crush, You, and Looking for You.

    They also collaborated with an artist named DreamDoll on a song titled Splash in October 2018. In 2019, Sianney and her twin sister also released songs such as Forever, Crazy Love, and Watch Me Go, featuring a rapper Miky Woodz.
