Sonia Agarwal is an Indian actor and model who has mainly worked in Tamil cinema as well as in some Telugu, Malayalam, and Kannada films. Her filmography consists of projects like Kaadhal Kondein, Nee Premakai, Madhurey, 7G Rainbow Colony, Chandu, Oru Kalluriyin Kathai, Palakkattu Madhavan, 7G Brindhavan Colony, Temper, Jamna Pyari, Ooh La La, Success, Dham, Kovil, Oru Naal Oru Kanavu, Theetta Rappai, Queen, Miya, Thanimai, Thiruttu Payale, Pudhupettai, Sadhurangam, Vaanam, Winner, Oru Nadigaiyin Vaakkumoolam, Ranna, Gruhanathan, Amma Nanna Oorelithe, Thadam, Red, and Dasharatha. In addition to that, she has also worked on a few television shows such as Naanal, Maali, Achcham Thavir, Paandavar Illam, Poove Unakkaga, and Nenaithale Inikkum.

    Sonia Agarwal Facts

  • Her mother tongue is Punjabi.
  • In 2002, Sonia Agarwal made her first Telugu-language film appearance as Raaji in the romance film Nee Premakai.
  • She made her Kannada film debut as Vidya in the 2002 romantic drama film Chandu. She co-starred in the movie alongside Sudeep.
  • Alongside Dhanush and Sudeep, Sonia Agarwal co-starred as Divya in the 2003 Tamil-language romantic psychological thriller film Kaadhal Kondein. This film marked her Tamil debut as well as won her the Filmfare Award for “Best Female Debut (South)” and the International Tamil Film Award for “Best New Actress”.
  • In 2004, she brought to life the character of Anitha in the romantic drama film 7G Rainbow Colony and this performance got Sonia Agarwal nominated for a Filmfare Award in the “Best Tamil Actress” category.
  • She was cast as Lakshmi (Ezhil and Kavin’s mother) in the 2019 Tamil-language crime thriller film Thadam. The film became one of the highest-grossing films of the year in Tamil Nadu.
