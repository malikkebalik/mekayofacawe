Surviving Summer

The Netflix debut of the Australian teen drama television series Surviving Summer took place on June 3, 2022. There are ten episodes in total in the first season. The series' central plot centers around Summer Torres (Sky Katz), a disobedient Brooklyn girl forced to live with a family and friend in the small Australian hamlet of Shorehaven along the Great Ocean Road. Summer tries her hardest not to love the town, the people, or the surf, but she can't help it.
