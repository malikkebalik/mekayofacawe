Who is Trevor Donovan?

Trevor Donovan is an American model and actor. He is best known for his role as Teddy Montgomery on the American teen drama television series 90210 (2009-2013).

He is also popular for his role as Elvis Presley on the CMT original series Sun Records (2017).

Trevor Donovan: Birth Facts, Family, Childhood, Education

Trevor Donovan was born Trevor Donovan Neubauer on 11 October 1978, in Bishop, California, USA. His nationality is American and he is of German Jewish ethnicity.

He was raised in Mammoth Lakes, California. He has a younger brother named Jake Donovan, who is a fireman in Sacramento.

Trevor Donovan knows how to ski and snowboard, also he was a part of the US teen ski team. He can also sing and play the guitar. He loves dogs since childhood.

Donovan graduated with a Bachelors’s Degree in Graphic Design from The Art Institute of Los Angeles.

Trevor Donovan: Professional Life, Career

Donovan has been an active member of the industry since 2004. His first acting role was as a Waiter in an episode of the popular American sitcom Quintuplets (2004).

His first movie role was as The Surrogate of Thomas Greer in the 2009 movie Surrogates. In June 2009, Donovan landed a recurring role in the American teen drama television series 90210 (2009-2013) as Teddy Montgomery. He was upgraded to series regular in May 2010.

In July 2011,Trevor Donovan was signed for a role in Oliver Stone’s film Savages (2012). He played the role of Matt in the film. In 2013, he landed a recurring role in the third season of Melissa & Joey (2013-2014).

Moreover, in 2014, he appeared as Trip Oliver in the film Bermuda Tentacles (2014). In 2017, he has appeared as Eddy Arnold in four seasons of Sun Records (2017).
