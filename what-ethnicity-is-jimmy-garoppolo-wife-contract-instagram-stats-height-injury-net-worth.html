The Ethnicity of Jimmy Garoppolo

Jimmy Garoppolo hails from a diverse background. He was born on November 2, 1991, in Arlington Heights, Illinois, USA. Jimmy’s roots are firmly grounded in his family. His father, Tony Garoppolo Sr., worked as an electrician, and his mother, Denise Garoppolo, was a homemaker. Jimmy Garoppolo Education and Early Football Passion

Jimmy attended Rolling Meadows High School, where he first discovered his passion for football. He played as both a quarterback and linebacker for the Mustangs football team. His high school career saw him make 25 touchdowns and pass for an impressive 3,136 yards.
