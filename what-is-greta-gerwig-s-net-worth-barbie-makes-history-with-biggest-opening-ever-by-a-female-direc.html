SACRAMENTO, CALIFORNIA: Greta Gerwig's 'Barbie' took the weekend by storm, bringing the beloved Mattel characters Barbie and Ken from their Dream House into the real world. The film surpassed expectations and grossed an impressive $155 million in the US and Canada on the opening weekend, making it the largest opening of all time for a female director. The global box office figures were equally astounding, with 'Barbie' raking in an astonishing $337 million in just three days.

'Barbie's opening also broke two other significant records of 2023. Firstly, it secured the highest domestic opening of the year, surpassing 'The Super Mario Bros' $146M collection. Additionally, 'Barbie' also dethroned 'The Super Mario Bros' for the largest-grossing day of 2023, bringing in $70.8 million compared to Mario's $54.8M.
