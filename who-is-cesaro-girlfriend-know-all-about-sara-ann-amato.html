Claudio Castagnoli (born December 27, 1980) is a Swiss professional wrestler who is most known for his time in WWE as Cesaro. Castagnoli competed in Ring of Honor (ROH) and indie organizations like Pro Wrestling Guerrilla (PWG), Combat Zone Wrestling (CZW), and Pro Wrestling Noah under his true name (PWN).

Cesaro is a successful tag team wrestler, having won the ROH World Tag Team Championship twice with his partner Chris Hero. Castagnoli began his wrestling career in his birthplace, under the tutelage of fellow Swiss wrestler SigMasta Rappo, and made his debut on December 24, 2000, in Essen, Germany, for Westside Xtreme Wrestling.

He began with a Japanese gimmick before switching to a Swiss financier, which he and Ares dubbed Swiss Money Holding. Dave Taylor provided Castagnoli with instruction when he was performing in England.
