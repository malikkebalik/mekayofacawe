SOUTH CAROLINA, CHARLESTON: ‘Southern Charm’ star Taylor Ann Green has recently revealed her relationship with a man named, Gaston, but she's maintained a discreet approach on social media

In one instance, she mentioned that he was TJ Dinch's roommate, offering limited details about him. Taylor went official on November 8 and shared a picture with Gaston, but his face wasn't fully visible.

This cautious sharing had sparked curiosity among fans, creating an air of mystery around Taylor's new beau.

Sharing how they met, she said, "I am dating, so now I know what I want in life," Taylor told US Weekly and shared that his name was Gaston.

She added, "I was like, ‘Unless he’s a closeted straight, I’m not dating Wilson.’ So, it’s TJ’s new roommate, and they actually started living together a while back. But that’s how I met him."

'Sothern Hospitality' cast did not approve of Taylor Green dating Gaston

The conversation unfolded, painting Gaston as someone embodying traits reminiscent of Taylor's past romantic experiences. 

"I want the best for her. I feel like she seems really happy and in a better place and I love Taylor,” Maddi, 26, said, while Joe called Taylor a “salt of the earth” girl and “an amazing person.”

Taylor Green broke up with Shep Rose as he feared ‘marriage’
