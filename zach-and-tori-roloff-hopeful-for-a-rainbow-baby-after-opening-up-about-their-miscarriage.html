Zach and Tori Roloff Hopeful for a Rainbow Baby after Opening up about Their Miscarriage

 Posted on September 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  472 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Zach Roloff and Tori Roloff are proud parents to two kids — son Jackson and daughter Lilah. The reality stars were expecting their third child but suffered a miscarriage back in March.

Roloff said she had never felt a loss as she did when she learned she and Zach had lost their third child. However, despite the tragic miscarriage, the reality stars are still hopeful that they will get to have another baby in the future.

Zach and Tori Roloff's Miscarriage

Zach and Tori shared they were expecting a third child when she was six weeks into her pregnancy in March.

The couple also revealed that they hadn’t yet had their first appointment with an obstetrician and were not planning on finding out whether their child was a boy or a girl, since they already had one each. 

They were planning on sharing the news with the world in March when Zach and Tori found out she had a miscarriage. Tori took to Instagram to talk about the loss, saying she found out about it after she went in for her first ultrasound at 8 weeks.

I’ve honestly never felt loss like I did in that moment. I’ve never felt so sad, angry, and scared in a single moment. I had no symptoms of losing out sweet baby angel and nothing could have prepared me for hearing our sweet baby was gone.

Tori also added that Zach had been with her the entire time and had acted as her support. She also noted that she and her husband were already blessed with two wonderful kids and the miscarriage had made her realize that fact.

Zach and Tori Roloff Hopeful For The Future

Despite the loss, Zach and Tori haven’t lost heart and are still looking forward to having more kids in the future. Zach had previously shared his and Tori’s family plans, stating they wanted a little pack.

The two shared they were not in a rush to have another baby and would let things proceed naturally, as they were happy right now. Tori also added that they were open to adoption.

I’ve always thought parents that have a heart for adoption or for foster care, I respect and adore them. I think those parents are amazing. It’s not something we’ve necessarily talked about, but those parents that do do that, I think it’s incredible.

Jackson And Lilah

Zach and Tori welcomed their first child, son Jackson Roloff, in May 2017.

Two years later, the Little People Big World stars’ family expanded when daughter Lilah Roloff was born in November 2019.

Jackson inherited achondroplasia, a type of short-limbed dwarfism, from his dad.

Zach and Tori understand the struggles their son might have to go through because of it and have vowed to raise him in such a way that the achondroplasia won’t stop him from living his best life.
